About Us

Welcome to Everest Grill & Restro in the heart of Tallinn! Our chefs, with over 20 years of international experience, bring authentic Asian, Thai, Indian, and Chinese flavors to every dish. From vibrant curries to savory stir-fries and special grilled Shaslik meats, our diverse menu has something for everyone.

Join us for an exceptional dining experience in an inviting atmosphere. We look forward to serving you!

  • Skilled Chefs

    Enjoy our vibrant space, where colorful ambiance and lively atmosphere create the perfect setting for memorable moments.
